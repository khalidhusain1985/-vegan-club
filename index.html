About Our Charity

Have you ever considered the effect of your choices on the animals, environment, and humans around us?

Although we are exposed at an early age to the prevalent attitude of using animals for food or entertainment, many end up living and experiencing it around us every day, including myself. That raises the very fundamental question to us all; why should animals have rights? This question marks the origin of a more compassionate world.

No living being on this planet deserves to be treated badly and those innocent creatures need our support. Trust and innocence are all that we can see in their eyes. We shall never fail this trust. So learned choices can make a difference and stop animal suffering as well as prevent environmental destruction.

Hello! Welcome to the Vegan Club. We were established in the year 2021 with the sole purpose of advocating for the establishment and protection of animal rights. Our organization is based on the simple belief that animals should never be used as experimental subjects, food items, and other entertainment purpose. We are here to support animal welfare and educate people to treat animals with kindness. Our club is dedicated to making the world kinder through veganism. It encompasses delicious food, a green planet, and ethical decisions that favor not only animals but also humans and the next generations.

Our Mission

The Vegan Club aims to educate and encourage people on how to live healthy, sustainable, and compassionate lifestyles since 2021. We promote a lifestyle that excludes, as far as possible and practical, all forms of exploitation of - and cruelty to - animals for food, clothing, or anything else. We want veganism as a means to reduce animal suffering, and environmental degradation and maintain better health.

Our Vision

The Vegan Club envisions a planet where animals are not utilized by human beings either for entertainment or food, and the world is in harmony. We are working hard to make it easy for people to use veganism as a means to reduce animal suffering, and environmental degradation and also to maintain better health. Changing a world into live healthy, sustainable, and compassionate lifestyles for all beings..
