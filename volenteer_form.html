Volunteer Details




Term & condition

By joining the Hello Vegan Club as a volunteer, you agree to the following terms and conditions that guide our community's mission and values:

  1. 1. Volunteers must commit to promoting veganism and its ethical principles.
  2. 2. All actions and communications must align with the club's mission and values.
  3. 3. Respect and inclusivity are mandatory in all interactions with the community.
  4. 4. Volunteers are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of club information.
  5. 5. The club reserves the right to terminate volunteer participation if guidelines are breached.
  6. 6. Volunteers are expected to contribute actively and positively to all events and initiatives.
  7. 7. Any use of the club's resources must be authorized and for club-related purposes only.
  8. 8. The club is not liable for any personal actions taken outside the scope of volunteering activities.

About Us

Vegan Club supports animal welfare and educates people to treat animals with kindness to make the world a happier place.

Our club is committed to promoting a gentler world through veganism, which entails tasty food, an environmental-friendly planet, as well as ethical choices in favor of animals Join us as we journey towards living more humanely.
